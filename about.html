About ICTRPBS 2026

Advancing Sustainable Construction, Building Maintenance, and Digital Infrastructure through BIM and Green Technology.

Overview

The International Conference on Civil Engineering & Building Maintenance (TRPBS) (ICTRPBS) is organized annually by Program Studi D4 Teknologi Rekayasa dan Pemeliharaan Bangunan Sipil, Fakultas Vokasi, Universitas Negeri Malang. Now in its 3rd edition, ICTRPBS 2026 brings together researchers, educators, industry practitioners, and policymakers to share advances in civil construction, road and bridge maintenance, green building, and digital construction technology. The 2026 edition will be held in hybrid format on November 18–19, 2026 in Malang, Indonesia.

Objectives

Knowledge Exchange

Provide a forum for researchers and practitioners to present original work in civil engineering and building maintenance.

Industry Collaboration

Strengthen links between vocational education institutions and construction industry partners.

Sustainable Innovation

Promote research addressing green building, sustainable infrastructure, and digital construction technology.

Publication & Indexing

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the conference proceedings and submitted for indexing in IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science and Scopus. Selected outstanding papers will be invited to extend their work for publication in partner journals.
