About ICSAET 2026

International Conference on Sustainable Advanced Engineering and Technology.

Overview

ICSAET 2026 is organized by Fakultas Vokasi, Universitas Negeri Malang, in partnership with Universiti Teknologi MARA, INTI International University, Universiti Malaysia Perlis, and KOSEN. The conference brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders working on sustainable and advanced engineering, in alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals. ICSAET 2026 will be held on September 23, 2026, at Universitas Negeri Malang, Indonesia.

Focus Areas

Sustainable Infrastructure

Built environment and infrastructure engineering aligned with sustainability goals.

Energy & Electronics

Sustainable energy, power systems, smart electronics, and intelligent systems.

Mobility & Manufacturing

Intelligent mobility, automotive engineering, and advanced manufacturing.

Publication & Indexing

Accepted papers are published in a Copernicus-indexed proceeding. Journal sponsor and partner outlets include APJI, Aritekin, Jurnal Pendidikan Teknik Bangunan (JPTB), LPKD, Journal of Vocational Studies, and Meratech (additional fee applies for publication in selected partner journals). The conference and its outlets are indexed in Google Scholar, Index Copernicus International, GARUDA, SINTA, Scilit, Crossref, Academia, and WorldCat.
