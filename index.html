About ICSAET 2026

ICSAET brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders working across sustainable infrastructure, energy, electronics, mobility, manufacturing, and technology management. Organized by Fakultas Vokasi, Universitas Negeri Malang, the conference is held in partnership with Universiti Teknologi MARA, INTI International University, Universiti Malaysia Perlis, and KOSEN. Accepted papers are published in a Copernicus-indexed proceeding.
