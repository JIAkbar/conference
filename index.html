About ICCEBM 2026

ICCEBM is an annual international forum organized by Program Studi D4 Teknologi Rekayasa dan Pemeliharaan Bangunan Sipil, Fakultas Vokasi, Universitas Negeri Malang, bringing together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders working on civil construction, road and bridge maintenance, green building, and Building Information Modeling (BIM). Accepted papers will be submitted for indexing in IOP Conference Series: Earth and Environmental Science and Scopus.
